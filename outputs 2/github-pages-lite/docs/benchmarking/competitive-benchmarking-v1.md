# Sahajeevanam Puram – Competitive / Comparative Benchmarking (V1)

## Purpose

This note benchmarks Sahajeevanam Puram against relevant precedent fragments to improve external credibility. These are not direct substitutes for the project. They are reference models for specific dimensions.

## 1. Auroville

### What it demonstrates

- experimental township imagination,
- values-led settlement building,
- long-horizon place identity,
- human-unity framing.

### What it does not provide

- accessibility-first infrastructure template,
- formal economic-zone logic,
- digital public operating-system layer,
- state-style special-zone finance structure.

### Why it matters to Sahajeevanam Puram

It shows that India can hold space for ambitious township visions, but Sahajeevanam Puram needs a more structured delivery and governance model.

## 2. GIFT City

### What it demonstrates

- state-backed flagship positioning,
- concentrated infrastructure strategy,
- special-zone governance logic,
- institutional and investor legibility.

### What it does not provide

- disability inclusion model,
- elder-mentor economy,
- accessibility-centered urban identity,
- care-linked public innovation.

### Why it matters to Sahajeevanam Puram

It provides the clearest Indian precedent for how a state-backed flagship zone can be structured and sold to institutions.

## 3. Enabling Village, Singapore

### What it demonstrates

- integrated disability inclusion,
- accessible public space,
- training and employment-linked programming,
- community infrastructure designed around diverse abilities.

### What it does not provide

- large-scale township model,
- special-zone governance,
- state-level land and capital stack complexity,
- 1,000-acre development ambition.

### Why it matters to Sahajeevanam Puram

It is the strongest precedent fragment for showing that accessibility-led place-making can be physical, social, and economically meaningful.

## 4. Synthesis

Sahajeevanam Puram is strongest when positioned as:

- Auroville-like in ambition,
- GIFT-like in governance seriousness,
- Enabling-Village-like in accessibility integration.

## 5. Differentiation

Compared with these precedents, Sahajeevanam Puram is differentiated by:

- combining accessibility with township-scale planning,
- linking elder mentorship and disability inclusion in one system,
- proposing a digital lead layer through Mopus City-OS,
- turning accessibility into a long-term economic flywheel,
- making universal design a central development principle rather than a compliance feature.

## 6. How to Use This in Pitches

### Government

Use benchmarking to show that the project is unusual but not unserious.

### Investors

Use benchmarking to show that there are recognizable precedent components, even if the total combination is new.

### Institutions

Use benchmarking to frame the project as a living laboratory with learnings from multiple validated models.
