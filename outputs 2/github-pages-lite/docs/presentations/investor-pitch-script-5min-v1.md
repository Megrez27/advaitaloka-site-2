# Investor Pitch Script

## Purpose

Use this script for a `5-minute` conversation with impact investors, catalytic capital providers, blended-finance participants, and PPP-aligned capital partners.

## Target Outcome

Secure:

1. interest in `Phase 0` catalytic participation,
2. follow-up diligence conversations,
3. alignment on post-Phase 0 structuring instead of premature underwriting.

## Opening

Sahajeevanam Puram is not yet a standard township deal, and we are not pretending that it is.

It is a staged inclusive infrastructure platform designed around accessibility, elder contribution, autonomous mobility, digital public systems, and future exportable revenue engines.

The key question is not whether the full township is underwritable today. The key question is whether `Phase 0` can de-risk the model enough to create a serious future platform opportunity.

## The Problem

Most accessibility and care systems are built as permanent cost centers.

This project starts from a different premise:

if housing, mobility, participation, mentorship, and digital work are designed together, then accessibility can become a productive operating advantage rather than only a welfare expense.

## The Model

The proposal combines four layers:

1. zero-barrier housing and universal design,
2. autonomous mobility and accessible public infrastructure,
3. Mopus City-OS and identity-linked service orchestration,
4. long-term revenue engines through digital workforce, mentorship, platform services, and inclusive design IP.

## Why It Is Interesting Financially

There are three reasons this is investable in stages.

First, the project has multiple future revenue engines, not only one.

Second, those engines do not need to start at the same time. Digital workforce and platform services can begin earlier than IP and premium mentorship exports.

Third, the package is now modeled through conservative, base, and upside cases, which creates a more serious conversation around sequencing and downside protection.

## What We Are Not Claiming

We are not claiming current bankability.

We are not claiming contracted revenues.

We are not claiming that the `INR 5,200 crore` vision envelope is final cost.

Those are exactly the reasons Phase 0 matters.

## What Phase 0 Does

The immediate ask is `INR 22 crore`.

That funds:

- SPV and pathway design,
- land and survey work,
- EIA and accessibility-led planning,
- technical and financial feasibility,
- and the demonstration layer that makes the project legible to government, capital, and partners.

In other words, Phase 0 converts a compelling vision into a reviewable and eventually financeable structure.

## Why Catalytic Capital Fits

Catalytic capital is the right first fit because the value at this stage is in de-risking:

- validating demand,
- testing governance,
- refining land and planning assumptions,
- shaping the first pilot,
- and clarifying how public, philanthropic, and commercial capital can later stack together.

## Close

The near-term question is not:

`Will you underwrite the whole township today?`

The right question is:

`Do you want to help shape the earliest de-risking stage of what could become a flagship inclusive infrastructure platform?`

That is the conversation we are inviting.

## Delivery Notes

- Keep the pace brisk and confident.
- Do not oversell certainty.
- Use the phrase:
  `staged platform opportunity`
- If challenged on revenue, answer:
  `That is why we built the model around scenarios and why we are keeping the first ask bounded.`
