# Chairperson Briefing Note

## Subject

Sahajeevanam Puram – Advaita-Loka | Executive briefing for chairperson-level review

## What This Is

Sahajeevanam Puram is a proposed `1,000-acre` `AI-enabled Accessibility Township`, `Inclusive Innovation Zone`, and `SPV-led Demonstration District` for Uttar Pradesh. It is designed around elder inclusion, Divyangjan independence, accessibility-first infrastructure, autonomous mobility, and digital public systems.

## What Decision Is Being Sought Now

The present request is not full sanction of the long-term `INR 5,200 crore` concept envelope.

The decision sought now is:

1. structured `Phase 0` review,
2. nodal ownership and pathway alignment,
3. permission to progress SPV, land, EIA, accessibility, and feasibility work,
4. return after Phase 0 for sharper DPR-grade and capital-stage review.

## Why It Matters

The proposal addresses three linked issues:

1. underused capability and rising loneliness among retired professionals,
2. mobility and employment barriers faced by Divyangjan,
3. fragmented delivery across care, housing, accessibility, and opportunity.

It aims to convert accessibility from a welfare-only lens into a productive development framework.

## Why Uttar Pradesh

As a legacy official benchmark, Uttar Pradesh recorded `4,157,514` persons with disabilities in Census 2011-based references, approximately `2.08%` of the state's population. This is already enough to justify state-level relevance, while current demand should be refreshed in Phase 0.

## What Makes It Distinct

- zero-barrier housing and universal design,
- autonomous mobility and accessible civic infrastructure,
- elder mentorship and contribution systems,
- Mopus City-OS and Sahajeevanam Card as digital lead infrastructure,
- long-term economic logic through digital workforce, mentorship exports, platform services, and inclusive design IP.

## Immediate Ask

### Phase 0 ask

- `INR 22 crore`

### What it covers

- SPV and regulatory structuring,
- land strategy and surveys,
- EIA and planning,
- accessibility-first masterplanning,
- technical, governance, and financial feasibility,
- demonstration-layer package for institutional review.

## Financial Caution

The `INR 5,200 crore` number should be treated as a concept-stage planning envelope, not as finalized project costing. The current financial model is scenario-based and intended to justify Phase 0 review, not to substitute for DPR-grade costing or investor underwriting.

## Why The First Decision Is Safe

The proposal is stage-gated. The state is not being asked to take blind capital risk now. Phase 0 is specifically designed to test land, demand, costing, governance, and implementation assumptions before any larger commitment.

## Recommended Chairperson-Level Direction

Support:

1. formal routing for structured review,
2. nodal ownership or coordination mechanism,
3. Phase 0 pathway design,
4. return with Phase 0 outputs before larger sanction decisions.

## Suggested One-Line Framing

`This is not a full-sanction request. It is a bounded Phase 0 review request for a flagship accessibility-led development model.`
