# Sahajeevanam Puram – Government Submission Pack (V1)

## 1. Submission Purpose

This pack is intended to support early-stage government review of `Sahajeevanam Puram – Advaita-Loka` as an `AI-enabled Accessibility Township`, `Inclusive Innovation Zone`, and `SPV-led Demonstration District` for Uttar Pradesh.

## 2. Decision Requested at This Stage

The current request is not full project sanction. The requested decision is:

1. acknowledge the strategic relevance of the proposal,
2. permit a structured `Phase 0` review process,
3. support SPV and delivery-pathway design,
4. review post-Phase 0 outputs before any large-scale capital sanction.

## 3. Strategic Case in One View

The project is designed to address three converging public needs:

1. rising underuse of retired professional capacity and elder isolation,
2. mobility and employment barriers faced by Divyangjan,
3. fragmented delivery across care, housing, accessibility, and local opportunity.

The proposed response is a `1,000-acre` integrated district where housing, mobility, work, learning, and governance are designed together instead of delivered through isolated schemes.

## 4. Why It Matters to Uttar Pradesh

- It aligns with `Atmanirbhar Bharat`, inclusive infrastructure, and future-ready service innovation.
- It creates a flagship accessibility model that can strengthen Uttar Pradesh’s policy and standards leadership.
- It introduces a replicable demonstration approach rather than asking the state to underwrite an untested city-scale rollout immediately.
- It creates visible employment and participation pathways for elders, Divyangjan, and youth operators.

## 5. Need Baseline

As a legacy official benchmark, Uttar Pradesh recorded `4,157,514` persons with disabilities in Census 2011-based references, approximately `2.08%` of the state's population. This is sufficient to establish state-level relevance. Phase 0 should refresh this with current addressable-demand, district-level targeting, and beneficiary segmentation.

## 6. Project Summary

Sahajeevanam Puram is proposed as a civic and economic ecosystem built around:

- `Aranya`: hydro-farms, sensory landscapes, healing environments,
- `Griha`: zero-stair universal-design homes,
- `Vidya`: Lyceum-led mentoring and learning systems,
- `Vyapaar`: digital work, maker-space production, and export-oriented activity,
- `Mopus City-OS`: the digital lead layer for identity, payments, telemetry, service coordination, and oversight.

## 7. Public-Interest Outcomes Expected

- increased mobility independence and accessibility confidence,
- reduced social isolation among elders,
- stronger employment participation for Divyangjan and youth,
- a standards-ready model for inclusive district design,
- a visible demonstration platform for policy, investor, and institutional partnerships.

## 8. Recommended Delivery Vehicle

The preferred route is a `state-supported SPV` with ring-fenced responsibility for:

- preparatory studies,
- partner onboarding,
- phased procurement design,
- governance and audit setup,
- stage-gated implementation control.

## 9. Phase 0 Scope

Phase 0 should be treated as a pre-implementation program, not as construction commencement.

### Phase 0 workstreams

1. SPV, regulatory, and institutional structuring
2. land strategy, screening, and site pathway
3. EIA, planning, and accessibility-led masterplanning
4. technical and financial feasibility
5. demand validation and beneficiary segmentation
6. demonstration-layer package for institutional review
7. PMO and stakeholder coordination

## 10. Phase 0 Budget and Immediate Ask

### Working ask

- `INR 22 crore`

### Suggested split

- SPV and legal structuring:
  `INR 2 crore`
- land and survey work:
  `INR 5 crore`
- EIA, planning, and accessibility design:
  `INR 4 crore`
- technical, governance, and financial feasibility:
  `INR 3 crore`
- demonstration layer and pilot materials:
  `INR 4 crore`
- PMO and stakeholder coordination:
  `INR 2 crore`
- contingency:
  `INR 2 crore`

## 11. Financial Framing for Review

The broader `INR 5,200 crore` development envelope should be treated as an illustrative concept-stage planning envelope. It should not be presented as finalized costing. The current revenue model is a scenario-based pitch support model built to justify feasibility and sequencing work, not to substitute for DPR-grade costing or investor underwriting.

## 12. Governance Safeguards

- privacy by design,
- accessibility-first oversight,
- explicit consent protocols,
- third-party audits,
- phased approvals,
- transparent operating standards,
- independent review of data and service guardrails.

## 13. Review Path Recommended

1. concept-stage acknowledgement and routing
2. interdepartmental review and nodal alignment
3. Phase 0 authorization
4. post-Phase 0 DPR and capital-readiness refinement
5. phase-gated implementation review
