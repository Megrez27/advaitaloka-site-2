# Sahajeevanam Puram – Advaita-Loka

## Government / DPIIT / State Consideration Note (DPR-Style V1)

## 1. Project Classification

Recommended approval-facing classification:

- AI-enabled Accessibility Township
- Inclusive Innovation Zone
- SPV-led Demonstration District

This terminology is recommended for formal review instead of "sovereign network state."

## 2. Project Overview

Sahajeevanam Puram – Advaita-Loka is a proposed 1,000-acre integrated township model in Uttar Pradesh designed around:

- zero-barrier housing,
- autonomous mobility,
- accessibility-first public infrastructure,
- AI-enabled governance and service orchestration through Mopus City-OS,
- productive inclusion of retired professionals, Divyangjan, and youth operators.

The project aims to reposition accessibility, dignity, and care as drivers of public value and economic productivity.

## 3. Public Problem Statement

The proposal responds to three converging public challenges:

1. underutilized knowledge and rising loneliness among retired professionals,
2. infrastructure-linked exclusion and limited employment pathways for Divyangjan,
3. fragmented care, low-access planning, and rising delivery costs in conventional urban systems.

As a legacy official benchmark, Uttar Pradesh recorded 4,157,514 persons with disabilities in Census 2011-based official references, around 2.08% of state population. This demonstrates that the scale of the challenge is already state-relevant, while actual current demand should be revalidated in Phase 0.

## 4. Strategic Rationale for Uttar Pradesh

The project is aligned to:

- Atmanirbhar Bharat priorities,
- accessibility-led infrastructure leadership,
- social inclusion through employment and mobility,
- state-level soft power and standards leadership,
- future-ready urban and social innovation.

## 5. Proposed Spatial Program

### Aranya – 600 acres

- hydro-farms,
- sensory gardens,
- Healing Lakes,
- ecological and therapeutic landscapes.

### Griha – 150 acres

- 5,000 universal design homes,
- zero-stair housing,
- tactile and voice-enabled accessibility.

### Vidya – 150 acres

- Lyceum mentoring spaces,
- holographic classrooms,
- elder-led teaching and learning systems.

### Vyapaar – 100 acres

- maker-spaces,
- digital workforce offices,
- export-linked activity,
- adaptive production and IP commercialization.

## 6. Digital Public Infrastructure Layer

The proposed digital backbone includes:

- Mopus City-OS,
- Sahajeevanam Card identity and contribution layer,
- Vines mobility-energy infrastructure,
- predictive service and maintenance telemetry,
- governance, audit, and oversight tools.

## 7. Institutional Delivery Model

### Recommended vehicle

State-supported SPV with ring-fenced delivery authority for:

- project planning,
- partner onboarding,
- capital mobilization,
- phased implementation,
- governance and accountability.

### Suggested public role

- enable Phase 0 authorization,
- support land pathway and regulatory coordination,
- define governance guardrails,
- anchor state legitimacy and interdepartmental alignment.

## 8. Phase 0 Scope

Phase 0 should be treated as a structured pre-implementation stage, not as construction commencement.

### Phase 0 components

- SPV and regulatory structuring,
- land strategy and site pathway,
- environmental impact assessment,
- masterplanning and accessibility design,
- financial and technical feasibility,
- demand validation,
- demonstration-layer development,
- stakeholder coordination.

## 10. Expected Public Outcomes

- increased accessibility and mobility independence,
- reduced social isolation among elders,
- new jobs and participation pathways,
- stronger state leadership in inclusive development,
- replicable accessibility and design standards,
- lower long-term systems fragmentation.

## 11. Governance and Trust Principles

- privacy by design,
- accessibility-first oversight,
- third-party audits,
- explicit consent systems,
- transparent operating standards,
- phased approval discipline.

## 12. Recommended Government Decision

The immediate recommendation is not full project sanction. The immediate recommendation is:

1. approve Phase 0 authorization,
2. support SPV and regulatory pathway design,
3. authorize preparatory studies and design work,
4. support cross-department coordination,
5. review post-Phase 0 outputs before full-scale capital commitment.
