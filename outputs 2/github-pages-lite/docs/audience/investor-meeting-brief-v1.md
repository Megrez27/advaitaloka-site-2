Position Sahajeevanam Puram as a high-conviction inclusive infrastructure platform with staged de-risking, multiple future revenue engines, and catalytic entry points before full capital formation.

## Primary Audience

- Impact investors
- catalytic capital partners
- PPP participants
- blended-finance and social-infrastructure investors

## Core Message

This is not yet an underwriting-ready township deal. It is a staged platform opportunity with:

1. early de-risking through Phase 0,
2. long-term upside through inclusive design IP, mentorship exports, and digital workforce revenues,
3. potential catalytic positioning for larger blended capital later.

## What To Emphasize In The Room

- staged entry instead of total capex shock
- multiple revenue engines with different ramp timing
- scenario discipline:
  conservative, base, and upside cases
- public-interest alignment plus exportable operating logic

## Questions You Should Be Ready For

1. Which revenue stream can actually start first?
2. How much of the value depends on state support?
3. What is de-risked in Phase 0?
4. How should catalytic and commercial capital be sequenced?
5. When does this become a bankable or investable structure?

## Recommended Close

Ask for catalytic participation in Phase 0 and post-Phase 0 structuring conversations rather than forcing premature underwriting of the full township.
