## Core Message

The project is a collaboration surface where partners can help shape:

1. accessibility standards,
2. digital public infrastructure,
3. education and mentorship systems,
4. demonstration pilots that can later scale.

## What To Emphasize In The Room

- this is a living lab, not just a township
- partners can participate before full build-out
- the project creates a visible flagship for inclusive innovation
- open standards and demonstrable pilots can produce outsized institutional influence

## Questions You Should Be Ready For

1. What can a partner do in Phase 0?
2. What is the first demonstrable pilot?
3. How is governance handled across institutions?
4. How are standards, ethics, and accessibility protected?
5. What visibility or strategic upside does a partner receive?

## Recommended Close

Ask for pilot collaboration, advisory participation, standards input, or demonstration-layer partnership rather than generic support.
